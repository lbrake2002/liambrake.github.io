About Me

Hello and thank you for taking the time to look at my ePortfolio. I created this website while I was a Student at Saskatchewan Polytechnic taking the Computer Systems Technology program. This website was created during my final year of taking the program with a graduation date in just over a month.

My name is Liam Brake and I will be graduating the Computer Systems Technology program in May 2024 at Saskatchewan Polytechnic. During the time I was taking the program I was working as a lifeguard for the City of Saskatoon were I still work to this day.

I was born and raised in Saskatoon where I still remain to this day and is where I will be receiving my diploma for the Computer Systems Technology program. I began my program in September 2021, and I continue to work hard to graduate in the Spring 2024 year!

Throughout my time in the Computer Systems Technology program I have gained the knowledge and skills to code in Java, C, C#, React, JavaScript and TypeScript languages. I also learned how to use the Bash and Perl scripting languages.

In my classes I learned on how to use Microsoft Excel and do Database management including using Microsoft Access and using the SQL language.
